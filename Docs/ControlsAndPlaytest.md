# Controls And Playtest

## Controls

- Tap a reachable floor tile to move along the dungeon path.
- Tap an enemy at close range to attack.
- Tap relic shards at close range to collect them.
- Tap the iron gate after restoring the relic shards.
- Tap a shrine at close range to restore health and mana.
- Tap `Potion` to consume a Crimson Tonic from the bag.
- If the Wanderer falls, tap `Potion` or a nearby shrine to rise.
- Tap `Bag` to view gold and loot.
- Tap `Codex` to view quest progress, story flags, and open storyline hooks.

## Chapter One Playtest Path

1. Start at the ruined belfry.
2. Move through the first crypt chamber.
3. Defeat the skeleton raiders and confirm the loot popup appears.
4. Open `Bag` and confirm potion, gold, and loot entries appear.
5. Move beside a shrine and confirm health and mana restoration appears.
6. Move beside Archivist Mael and trigger the archivist story beat.
7. Collect three violet relic shards.
8. Move beside the iron gate and open it.
9. Enter the obsidian arena.
10. Defeat the Ash Warlock.
11. Confirm `Chapter Complete: Ashes Beneath Black Hollow` appears.
12. Open `Codex` and confirm the Northern Citadel, Hollow Oath, and Three Roads hooks appear.

## Survival Playtest

1. Enter combat with low health.
2. Let an enemy counterattack until the Wanderer falls.
3. Confirm movement and attack taps show the rise prompt.
4. Tap `Potion` and confirm the Wanderer rises.
5. Repeat near a shrine and confirm shrine restoration clears the downed state.

## Offline Playtest

Run the chapter path with the device network radios disabled. The game uses bundled Swift, JSON, PNG, asset catalog, and storyboard resources.
